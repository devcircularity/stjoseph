Program Description :

Families living in poverty have limited resources for everyday needs like food and housing, leaving many children to rely on meals provided at school. For numerous children, the meal they receive in school is often the only meal they have for the day. Recognizing this critical issue, we established a Feeding Programme to provide additional meals for pupils who depend solely on school meals. This program also supports children with health conditions arising from inadequate diets.

Program Objectives :

Our Feeding Programme is designed to address and mitigate the challenges faced by children living in poverty, ensuring they receive the necessary nutrition for their health and well-being.

  • Provide nutritious meals to schoolchildren who depend on school for their daily sustenance.
  • Support children with health conditions due to poor diets with additional nutritional support.
  • Reduce absenteeism in school by ensuring children have access to at least one substantial meal a day.

Impact of the Programme :

Through the Feeding Programme, we aim to create a significant positive impact on the health and educational outcomes of the children we serve. The programme is a lifeline to many children, offering them hope and the energy to learn and thrive.

  • Improved nutrition and health status among schoolchildren.
  • Increased school attendance and reduced absenteeism.
  • Enhanced focus and performance in school due to better nutrition.
  • A foundation for healthier future generations.
